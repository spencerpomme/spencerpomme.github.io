Why basic BST will fail

After certain times of insertions and deletions, BST may devolve into a linked list structure, the time complexity changes from $O(\lg n)$ to $O(n)$ (for search operation).

The reason of which is due to the BST insertion behaviour. Every time insert a new into the BST, the newly inserted node is going to be a leaf. This implies a possible increase in height of the BST (increase by 1, to be specific).
